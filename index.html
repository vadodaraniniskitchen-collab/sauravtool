Payment Logic

1. On each payment, total due = previous pending + current day's category dues.

2. Payment is allocated to categories in order: Pickup → Housekeeping → Wiping.

3. If paid amount ≥ total due → all dues cleared, surplus becomes advance.

4. If paid amount < total due → partial payment recorded, remaining stays pending.

5. Advance from a previous payment reduces the next day's dues automatically.
